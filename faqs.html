Plan with Confidence

Select a topic below to review important information for your Taniti trip.

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency. Many businesses also accept euros and yen, and several banks provide currency exchange. Major credit cards are accepted by many businesses.

Power outlets on Taniti are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti. However, as tourism increases, visitors should still watch for petty crimes such as pickpocketing.

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Alcohol is not served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m. The drinking age on Taniti is 18.

Taniti has many national holidays. Some tourist attractions and restaurants may close on holidays, so visitors should plan ahead.
