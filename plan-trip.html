Build Your Taniti Plan

Start with the major decisions first, then review practical travel details before finalizing your trip.

Choose Lodging

Compare hotels, the resort, the hostel, bed and breakfasts, and family-owned stays.

2

Pick Activities

Explore beaches, rainforest hikes, the volcano, sightseeing, entertainment, and Merriton Landing.

3

Plan Transportation

Decide whether to use rental cars, buses, taxis, bikes, walking, or cruise and airport arrival options.

4

Plan Food & Dining

Review restaurant styles, supermarkets, grocery stores, and the 24-hour convenience store.

5

Review Visitor FAQs

Check currency, power outlets, safety, language, alcohol rules, medical services, and holidays.
