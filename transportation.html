Getting Around Taniti

Transportation

Plan how you will arrive on Taniti and how you will travel around the island after you get here.

Arrival and Ground Transportation

Visitors can use this page to compare arrival options and choose the best way to move between lodging, activities, dining, and sightseeing.

✈️

Airport Arrival

Most visitors arrive by air. Taniti has a small airport that serves small jets and propeller planes.

🚢

Cruise and Boat Travel

Cruise ship arrivals dock in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night each week. Visitors can also find boat tours as a sightseeing option while exploring the island.

🚗

Rental Cars

Rental cars are available from a local rental agency near the airport. This is a useful option for visitors who want flexibility while exploring the island.

🚌

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City every day from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. Private buses serve other areas of the island.

🚕

Taxis

Taxis are available in Taniti City and can help visitors travel between major city locations, lodging, and attractions.

🚲

Bikes and Walking

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors. Helmets are required by law. Taniti City is fairly flat and walkable.

Walkable Area

Merriton Landing

Many tourists stay near Merriton Landing, a developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. This area is easy to explore on foot, making it useful for visitors who want nearby dining, activities, and entertainment.

Planning Tip

First-time visitors should decide whether they want a rental car for island-wide exploring or whether they will stay mainly in walkable areas such as Taniti City and Merriton Landing.
