Eat and Shop on Taniti

Food & Dining

Find restaurant styles, grocery options, and convenient food planning information before arriving on the island.

Restaurants and Grocery Options

Taniti offers local dining, familiar meal options, Pan-Asian cuisine, supermarkets, small grocery stores, and a 24-hour convenience store.

🐟

Local Fish and Rice

Five restaurants serve mostly local fish and rice, giving visitors a chance to try food connected to Taniti’s island culture.

🍔

American-Style Meals

Three restaurants serve American-style meals for visitors who want familiar food options during their stay.

🍜

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Two restaurants serve Pan-Asian cuisine, giving visitors additional dining variety while planning meals.

🛒

Supermarkets

Taniti has two supermarkets for visitors who want to buy groceries, snacks, drinks, or supplies during their trip.

🥫

Small Grocery Stores

Two smaller grocery stores are available for quick food shopping and basic travel needs.

🌙

24-Hour Convenience Store

Taniti has one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day, which can help visitors who need food or supplies outside normal hours.

Meal Planning Tip

Plan meals around your activities

Visitors planning beach days, rainforest hikes, volcano visits, or tours may want to check nearby dining and grocery options before leaving for the day.

Helpful Reminder

Some restaurants and attractions may close during Taniti’s national holidays. Visitors should review FAQ information before finalizing daily plans.
