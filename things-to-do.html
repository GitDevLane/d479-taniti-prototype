Things to Do

Discover beaches, rainforest hikes, volcano visits, local tours, entertainment, and sightseeing options across Taniti.

Popular Activities and Attractions

Use this page to compare outdoor activities, sightseeing options, and entertainment areas before building your trip plan.

🏖️

Beaches

Taniti offers both sandy and rocky beaches. Many visitors spend time near the white sandy beaches around Yellow Leaf Bay.

🌿

Rainforest

Visitors can explore the tropical rainforest through hikes, zip-lining, and nature-focused sightseeing activities.

🌋

Volcano

Taniti has a mountainous interior with a small active volcano, making volcano visits one of the island’s major attractions.

🚤

Tours and Sightseeing

Popular sightseeing options include boat tours, bus tours, rainforest hikes, volcano visits, helicopter rides, and chartered fishing tours.

🎭

Entertainment

Visitors can find a museum, pubs, a microbrewery, a dance club, movies, an arcade, art galleries, and bowling.

📍

Merriton Landing

Many activities are located in Merriton Landing, a developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay that is easy to explore on foot.

Suggested First Stop

Start with Taniti City and Yellow Leaf Bay

Many tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, where visitors can experience native architecture and nearby white sandy beaches around Yellow Leaf Bay.

Planning Tip

First-time visitors should choose a mix of outdoor activities and practical planning. Consider beaches, the rainforest, the volcano, transportation needs, and holiday closures before finalizing plans.
