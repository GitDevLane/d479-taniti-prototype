Where to Stay

Lodging

Compare Taniti lodging options, from affordable stays to resort-style accommodations, before planning activities and transportation.

Choose a Stay That Fits Your Trip

Taniti offers several lodging choices for visitors, including hotels, a resort, hostels, family-owned hotels, and bed and breakfasts.

🏨

Hotels

Taniti has many small, family-owned hotels that give visitors a local lodging option near island activities and services.

🌴

Four-Star Resort

Visitors looking for a more traditional vacation experience can choose Taniti’s large four-star resort.

🎒

Inexpensive Hostel

Budget-focused travelers can consider the inexpensive hostel as a lower-cost option for staying on the island.

🛏️

Bed and Breakfasts

Taniti has a growing number of bed and breakfasts for visitors who want a smaller and more personal lodging experience.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family-Friendly Stays

Families can compare hotels, resort options, and bed and breakfasts based on location, comfort, and access to activities.

✅

Inspected Lodging

All types of lodging are regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Planning Location

Stay Near What You Want to Do

First-time visitors should choose lodging based on the activities they want to prioritize. Taniti City, Yellow Leaf Bay, and Merriton Landing can help visitors stay close to beaches, dining, sightseeing, and entertainment.

Planning Tip

After choosing lodging, review transportation options so you know whether you will need a rental car, taxi, bus, bike, or walkable route during your stay.
